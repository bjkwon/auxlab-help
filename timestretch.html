timestretch

y = timestretch ( x,factor )
y = x.timestretch ( factor )
y = x % factor

Stretch the audio in time without changing the pitch or the spectrum
arg type Description Unit or Value scale
xAUD Audio signal
factorSCL or TSQThe time stretch factor

Outputs
  • y is AUD or VCT

Notes
  • The audio extends in time by the factor of x--e.g., x means the new duration is 1.5 times the old one
  • Do not put a percentage value for x.
