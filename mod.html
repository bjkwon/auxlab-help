mod

y = mod ( x,div )
y = x.mod ( div )
y = x % div

Remainder after division (modulo operation)
arg type Description Unit or Value scale
xSCAL, VCT or AUDIO n/a
divSCALn/a

Outputs
  • y is the remainder after division of x by div

Notes

Examples
  • mod(17,5) rarr; 2
  • mod(-1,3) rarr; -1
  • (12).mod(5) rarr; 2
