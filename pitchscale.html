pitchscale

y = pitchscale ( x,factor )
y = x.pitchscale ( factor )
y = x # factor

Rescale the pitch of the audio without changing the duration
arg type Description Unit or Value scale
xAUD Audio signal
factorSCL or TSQThe pitch stretch factor

Outputs
  • y is AUD or VCT

Notes
  • For x=1.06 the pitch is adjusted up by one semitone.
  • Similiar to respeed, but the duration is retained in pitchscale
