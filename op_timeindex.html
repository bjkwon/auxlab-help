~ (time-indexing)

x(t1 ~ t2)

Portion of audio x from t1 ms to t2 ms
Commutative
N/A

Data Types
x t1 t2
AUDSCALSCAL

Notes
  • If t1 > t2, the extracted signal is time-reversed.

Examples
    Open a .wav file "spring" and extract from 527 ms to 633 ms.
    AUX> x=wave("spring");

    AUX> y=x(527~633);

    To time-reverse the sound,
    AUX> x=x(end~0);
