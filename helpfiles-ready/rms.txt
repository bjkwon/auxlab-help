[FUNCTION NAME] rms
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Calculates the rms energy in dB
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES] the signal 
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  In AUX, by definition, the rms of a sinusoid with the full magnitude is 0 dB.
[EXAMPLES] The rms of a sinusoid with the magnitude half of the full scale is -6 dB.
[SEE ALSO]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is SCAL
