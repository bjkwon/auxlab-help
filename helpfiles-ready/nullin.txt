[FUNCTION NAME] nullin
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Insert time markers in an audio signal
[PARAMETERS]  x p
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  AUD	TSEQ, VECT, or SCAL
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  audio signal
Time sequence with desired tmarks. Values of the sequence are ignored. 
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
n/a
[NOTES]  #p1 can be a vector with time points in milliseconds.
[EXAMPLES]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[SEE ALSO]  contig
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is an audio signal from #p1 with time markers as specified.
