[FUNCTION NAME] toc
[STATIC] 1
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION] Read elapsed time from stopwatch
[PARAMETERS]  
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES] 
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  
[NOTES] no argument (Windows only)
[EXAMPLES]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[SEE ALSO]  tic
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
