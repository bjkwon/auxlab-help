[FUNCTION NAME] angle
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Returns the phase angles
[PARAMETERS]  Z
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  SCAL or VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  complex value(s)
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  
[SEE ALSO]  abs atan
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is the phase angles of #p1 in radian.
