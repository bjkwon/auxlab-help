[FUNCTION NAME] ceil
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Round toward positive infinity
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]    SCAL, VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  n/a
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  ceil(-2.3) returns -2.
ceil(2.3) return 3.
[SEE ALSO]  fix floor round
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is an "always-round-up" version of #p1.
