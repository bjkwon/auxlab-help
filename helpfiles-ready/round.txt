[FUNCTION NAME] round
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Round to nearest decimal or integer
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]    SCAL, VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  n/a
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  round(2.6) &rarr; 3
round(2.4) &rarr; 2
round(-2.6) &rarr; -3
round(-2.4) &rarr; -2
[SEE ALSO]  ceil fix floor
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is a "half-round-up" version of #p1.
