[FUNCTION NAME] noise
[STATIC] 1
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION] Generate a white noise signal
[PARAMETERS] duration
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB] SCAL
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES] duration of the signal to generate
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES] millisecond
[NOTES] The random signal is generated from a uniform distribution.
[EXAMPLES]
[SEE ALSO]  gnoise
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is uniform white noise (random numbers from a uniform distribution) with amplitude bound between -1 and 1.
