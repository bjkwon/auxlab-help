[FUNCTION NAME] imag
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Imaginary part of complex number
[PARAMETERS]  Z
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  SCAL or VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  complex value(s)
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  (3).imag &rarr; 0<br>(2+sqrt(-1)).imag &rarr; 1
[SEE ALSO]  conj real abs angle
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is the imaginary part of #p1
