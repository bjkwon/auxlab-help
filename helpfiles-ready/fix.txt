[FUNCTION NAME] fix
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Round toward zero
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]    SCAL, VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  n/a
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES] fix(3.99) &rarr; 3 
fix(-3.99) &rarr; -3 
[SEE ALSO]  ceil floor round
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] If you take out the decimal portion of #p1, you get #q1.
