[FUNCTION NAME] sqrt
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]   Square root
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  SCAL or VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  n/a
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  If #p1 is non-audio, square root of negative values produce imaginary values. If #p1 is audio, this produces sign(sqrt(#p1)).
[EXAMPLES]  
[SEE ALSO]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is square root of #p1.
