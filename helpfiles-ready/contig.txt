[FUNCTION NAME] contig
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Fill in all null portions with zeros and make the audio signal contiguous.
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  AUD
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  audio signal
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[SEE ALSO] nullin 
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is a time-contiguous version of #p1, i.e., all null portions filled in with zeros.
