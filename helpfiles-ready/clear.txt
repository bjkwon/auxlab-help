[FUNCTION NAME] clear
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Clear the variable (or the member variable) from the workspace
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  any variable or a member variable of a class variable
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  a.clear or clear(a)&rarr; clear the variable a<br>a.obj.clear or clear(a.obj) &rarr; clear the member obj from the class variable a
[ALGORITHM]  
[SEE ALSO]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] 
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] No output
