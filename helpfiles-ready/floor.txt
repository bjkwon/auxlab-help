[FUNCTION NAME] floor
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Round toward negative infinity
[PARAMETERS]  x
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]    SCAL, VCT or AUDIO
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  n/a
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  n/a
[NOTES]  
[EXAMPLES]  floor(3.99) &rarr; 3
floor(-3.99) &rarr; -4
[SEE ALSO]  ceil fix round
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
[OUTPUT ARGS--MULTILINES] #q1 is a "round-down" version of #p1.
