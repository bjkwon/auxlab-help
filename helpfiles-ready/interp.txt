[FUNCTION NAME] interp
[STATIC] 0
[FUNCTION DESCRIPTION]  Convolute the two arrays
[PARAMETERS]  x y q
[PARAM TYPE DELIM BY TAB]  VCT	VCT	SCL or VCT
[PARAM DESCRIPTION--MULTILINES]  the x array
the y array
the proving value(s) that you want to interpolate
[PARAM UNIT--MULTILINES]  

n/a
[NOTES]  #p1 and #p2 must be the same length.
#p3 can be either a scalar or vector
[EXAMPLES]
[SEE ALSO]  
[ALGORITHM]  
[OUTPUT ARGS BY TAB] y
